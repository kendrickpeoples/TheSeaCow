TheSeaCow

The Sea Cow is a popular ice cream shop based out of Destin, Florida, on the beautiful Emerald Coast. It was founded by Jacob Montague in the summer of 2017 with a vision to be the first floating ice cream shop on the famous Crab Island sandbar. We offer a delightful selection of hand-scooped ice cream, milkshakes, smoothies, sno cones, Dippin' Dots, and Sea Cow souvenirs. Recently, in 2023, the ice cream boats on Crab Island were sold to Nicholas Bannano, and we expanded to an on-land operation to broaden our brand. Nick plans to continue the expansion by opening many more on-land storefronts as well as other boat locations. Join us for the best ice cream experience on both the Emerald Coast and Crab Island! Visit The Sea Cow ice cream shop today.
